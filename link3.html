Thinking ahead on this ranking, and Phoenix's position is the toughest to nail. The thing is...we don't get enough screentime showing Joaquin as the Joker, by design. He really is the archetype for the Joker we know today: a maniacal madman who is unafraid to be the spark that ignites the revolution. That said, his time as Joker isn't nearly as impressive as the journey he took getting there.
